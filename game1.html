Development time: 6 weeks

About the Game

Imagine running your own base where you can mine cool resources AND build awesome defense towers! It's like having a factory and a fortress all in one.

What makes it fun?

  • • Mine resources to power up your base
  • • Build towers to protect your precious Core
  • • Watch out for enemy spawners that roam around and team up with each other
  • • The longer you survive, the tougher it gets - enemies get stronger when their spawners merge together and become BIGGER!

Think of it as playing with building blocks, but with an exciting twist - you need to keep the bad guys away from your Core while gathering everything you need to build better defenses. The more you build, the stronger you get... but the enemies are getting stronger too!

Game Mechanics

Mine Resources!

There's only one resource collector available, so you must use a belt system to transport all the resources

Build towers!

Protect your base by building different types of towers. The more resources it requires to build the better the tower!

Electric tower

This tower zaps enemies in its range up to 3 enemies at once! It chooses the closest enemies to the first target!

Red Ore Mines

You might even need to utilize resources to defend your base. This system places the mines created from red ore, which, when stepped on, will explode
